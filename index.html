About Deja View

College life is glamorized in movies that we watch, which raises our expectations when we enter the college for first time. However, we find out soon that our college life is not a tiny bit similar to those characters. Deja View, the movie club of the BBA programme, is all determined to break the dull routine of the students of Institute of Management. Nirma University. They are here to show different movies to the students. Their aim is to teach important lessons in way that the students enjoy it

This year we plan on bigger and better events, to hold about 3 movies evey month and targrt to complete 12 movies this term.

After the screening of the movie, we will organise a quick discussion on: The plot, climax, storyline and even going for possible alternative to the plot and climax.

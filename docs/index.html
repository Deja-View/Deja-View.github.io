About Deja View

The club intends to break the routine of the students by giving them a different perspective of the world by looking through the screen. We aims to teach important lessons in a way that the students enjoy it while giving the members a chance to understand and experience different aspects of event management and master it while coordinating the efforts with other members to build a strong team.

This year we plan on bigger and better events, to hold about 3 movies evey month and targrt to complete 12 movies this term.

After the screening of the movie, we will organise a quick discussion on: The plot, climax, storyline and even going for possible alternative to the plot and climax.
